Achievify

Curating a plan to create healthy habits.

A Mobile App Case Study

In this case study, I worked as a UX designer alongside 2 other UX prospective designers to create a task management mobile application integrated with ai technology.

User Research

To begin thinking of ways to tackle this issue, we began brainstorming ways that one could successfully attempt to improve their health habits. It could be helpful to set daily goals and reminders. People could develop their task management skills to implement small activities into their daily lives.

To better understand the prospective users of our app, we created a survey on Google Forms, and we also conducted 5 one-on-one interviews.

“I found the best results with tracking apps keeping track of calories, for example.”

-Quote from a User's Interview

“I like the idea of getting guidance in an app!”

-Quote from a User's Interview

Problem Statement

We believe that creating a wellness application offering curated daily tasks and ai-generated health plans for our users, like Lauren, who are navigating challenges with their mental and physical health, will achieve success in fostering a healthier lifestyle.
